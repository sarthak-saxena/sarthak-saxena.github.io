MakersFete'14

Makers Fete is a platform for all the change makers of the country to showcase and display change making ideas, creativity, inventions, resourcefulness and start-ups that in any way would help in making the world a better place to live in. Makers Fete is a gathering of tech enthusiasts, crafters, tinkerers, hobbyists, engineers, science clubs, students, and commercial exhibitors. It emphasizes to bring together a community of makers and give them a platform to showcase and exhibit their ideas, products and creations.
